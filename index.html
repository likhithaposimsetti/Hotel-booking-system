about us

Taj Hotels is a chain of luxury hotels and a subsidiary of the Indian Hotels Company Limited headquartered in Mumbai, India. Incorporated by Jamsetji Tata in 1902,the company is a part of the Tata Group. The company employed over 20,000 people in the year 2010. The company has been ranked as the World’s "Strongest Hotel" Brand in 2022and India’s Strongest Brand as per Brand Finance Hotels 50 Report 2022.
